Who I Am

My name is Zachary Allnutt. I live in Wake Forest, NC. I'm 25 years old and I'm studying Graphic Design at Wake Tech Community College. I have been a part of a lot of drawing classes and classes that teach InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator. I'm mostly into graphic design but I took web design classes because I thought it might be interesting to know how to build a website. I started drawing and doing other forms of art when I was in middle school and I decided then that I wanted to have a job in design/art when I grew up.

When I'm not doing school work and other things, I like to play video games, watch tv shows and movies, and play basketball. I also like to go to the beach and just sit on the beach and listen to music.

My Goals

My goal is to be a great graphic designer that does designing for big companies. I think that would be really cool if, one day, I get recognized for my designs and other things I do for these companies and maybe for myself.
